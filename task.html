University School of Engineering

University School of Engineering & Technology (USET) is one of the prominent school of LTSU offering distinctive cutting-edge skilling technical courses, which bridges the gap between skill-based higher education and employability. University School of Engineering & Technology encourages all-around development through industry designed curriculum focusing on enhancing both cutting-edge technical skills and soft skills. Strong emphasis on practical hands-on experience and Live projects so that students can directly be deployed in the industry as On Job Deployment (OJD). The courses are offered in collaboration with global tech giants to structure the curriculum, training students and making them industry-ready in skill-based courses, guiding them on live projects, mentored by leading industry experts and academicians. The aim of USET is to create a skill-based workforce, in various domains and futuristic technology areas.
